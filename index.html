About Our Company

Our Mission

We specialize in international trading of frozen seafood products, connecting importers, distributors, and processing plants worldwide with reliable suppliers and seamless logistics solutions.

What We Do

With 20+ years of experience, we facilitate full container load (40ft) transactions of premium frozen seafood products. Our business model focuses on trade facilitation and logistics coordination—we don't hold inventory, which allows us to offer competitive pricing and maintain flexibility for our clients.

Our Expertise

  • Extensive Supplier Network: Direct relationships with 300+ exporters across Latin America and other regions
  • Trade Compliance Consulting: Expert guidance on international documentation, certifications, and regulatory requirements
  • Risk Mitigation: Ensuring transaction security and preventing fraud in international seafood trade
  • Logistics Coordination: Managing the complete supply chain from source to destination port

Our Approach

We serve as your trusted partner in the complex world of international seafood trading. Our role is consultative—we ensure compliance, coordinate logistics, and connect you with the right suppliers while exporters handle the documentation process. This approach removes the hassle from sourcing and guarantees reliable, continuous supply.

Global Reach

We serve clients in over 40 countries, with particular expertise in the USA, China, Spain, Thailand, and Vietnam markets. Our established supplier relationships enable us to source approximately 20 different seafood products, all available in full container quantities.

Our Commitment

  • Competitive pricing through established supplier networks
  • Fast, reliable service and continuous supply
  • Transaction security and compliance assurance
  • Transparent communication throughout the process
